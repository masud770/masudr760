Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Your privacy is important to us at masud.aio.bd. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website, in compliance with global privacy standards and advertising requirements.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal and non-personal information when you visit or interact with our website, including:

  • Identity Data: Name or email addresses submitted via contact forms.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, device information, and operating system.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, pages visited, and time spent.
  • Cookies: Small files used to improve user experience and deliver personalized content.

2. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements on our website. To ensure compliance with Google's policies, please note the following:

  • Third-party Vendors: Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on masud.aio.bd.
  • DART Cookie: Google's use of the DoubleClick DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.
  • Opt-out: You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy.
  • Personalized Advertising: You can manage your ad preferences or opt out of personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

3. How We Use Information

The data we collect is used to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide technical support.
  • Optimize website performance and user interface.
  • Deliver relevant advertisements through the Google AdSense network.
  • Analyze traffic patterns to provide better cybersecurity and AI-related content.

4. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures (including SSL encryption) to protect your data. We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external sites (GitHub, LinkedIn, Blogger). We do not control the privacy practices of these third-party services and encourage you to read their respective policies.

6. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Bangladesh. Any disputes relating to these terms shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Bangladesh.
